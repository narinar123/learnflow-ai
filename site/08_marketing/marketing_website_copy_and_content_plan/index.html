1. Brand Voice & Tone

Attribute Description In Practice
Empowering We believe in our users’ potential “You’ve got this.” not “We’ll help you succeed.”
Smart & Friendly Expert knowledge, approachable delivery No jargon without explanation
Encouraging Celebrate small wins “Every lesson is a step forward.”
Clear & Direct No marketing fluff “Learn Python in 8 hours” not “Unlock your potential with our comprehensive Python journey”
Human Technology in service of people “AI that learns how you learn”

Voice Examples:

  • ✅ “Your AI tutor, always ready.”
  • ❌ “Revolutionary AI-powered paradigm shifting educational solutions.”
  • ✅ “500+ courses. 0 boring lessons.”
  • ❌ “A comprehensive catalog of expertly curated content across diverse domains.”

Q: Is there a student discount?

4. Paid Marketing Strategy

4.1 Google Ads

Campaigns:

  • Brand (brand protection): “LearnFlow AI”, “LearnFlow”
  • Category: “Python course online”, “Data science certification India”
  • Competitor: “Coursera alternative India”, “Udemy alternative India”

Budget Allocation:

  • Brand: 10%
  • Category: 60%
  • Competitor: 30%

Target CPA: ₹400 per free signup / ₹1,200 per Pro subscriber

4.2 Meta/Instagram Ads

Formats: Reels (primary), Stories, Feed
Audience targeting: 18–35, India, interests: technology, career, education, coding
Creative strategy: Learner transformation stories (before/after), AI demo videos, certificate unboxing

4.3 YouTube Pre-roll

Target: Tutorial content (Python, Excel, Design) viewers
Creative: 15-second non-skippable: “The AI tutor that explains what you just watched, better.”
